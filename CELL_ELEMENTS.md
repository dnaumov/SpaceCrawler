# Cell Elements

This document describes the current non-combat cell element catalog for the prototype.

## Movement

- **Core membrane** (required base): provides baseline integrity, energy capacity, and connector points.
- **Flagella engine**: increases movement speed, with higher energy drain.
- **Streamlined tail fin**: improves directional stability and turning efficiency.

## Sensors

- **Sensor cilia**: improves food targeting and collection range, at a small durability trade-off.
- **Chemical gradient receptor**: increases detection range for nearby food clusters.
- **Pulse scanner node**: periodically reveals high-density resource zones in a short radius.

## Energy

- **Mitochondria cluster**: increases max energy and food-to-energy conversion efficiency.
- **Storage vacuole**: increases temporary reserve capacity for burst movement phases.
- **Efficiency regulator**: lowers passive drain during low-activity moments.

## Auxiliary

- **Armor plate**: increases health/integrity at the cost of movement speed.
- **Buoyancy bladder**: improves maneuverability in vertical movement bands.
- **Stabilizer ring**: reduces collision slow-down and helps maintain movement momentum.

> Combat-related elements are intentionally excluded at this stage.
